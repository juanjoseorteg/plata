Apple Pay Shortcut
Set up automatic transaction logging in 5 minutes
1
Open Shortcuts → Automation → New Automation → Apple Pay
This trigger fires every time you complete an Apple Pay payment on your iPhone.
2
Add "Ask for Input" — type Number
Prompt: "How much did you spend?" — you type the amount right after paying.
3
Add "Choose from Menu" for category
Options: Food, Transport, Shopping, Health, Entertainment, Bills, Other.
4
Add "Open URL" with your Plata deep link
This opens the app and pre-fills the transaction form automatically.
plata://log?amount=[Provided Input]&cat=[Chosen Item]
5
Turn off "Ask Before Running"
So the Shortcut fires silently in the background every time you pay.
